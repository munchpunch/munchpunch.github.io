Delivery

Orders are currently despatched on Friday each week. Orders must be received by 3pm the day prior to delivery in order to leave time for freshly baking your items. Some items may require a longer turn- around. all perishable products are sent to you fresh and securely packed to arrive the following day - as such we are able to deliver only to near places.
- Your orders will arrive any time between 10am and 5:30 pm on your delivery day.
- Delivery is not guaranteed for any particular time during the day.
- Please let us know as soon as possible if your order has not arrived within the time frame.
-Please note that delivery dates may be affected by public or Bank holidays.
